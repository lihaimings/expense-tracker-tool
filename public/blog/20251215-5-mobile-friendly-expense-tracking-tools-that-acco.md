# 5 Mobile-Friendly Expense Tracking Tools That Accountants and Small Business Owners Love to Hate

Expense tracking is a crucial aspect of managing your personal or business finances. As accountants and small business owners, you know that keeping track of expenses can be tedious and time-consuming. Fortunately, there are several mobile-friendly expense tracking tools available that can help simplify this process.

In this article, we will explore five popular expense tracking tools that can save you time and streamline your financial management. We'll also provide practical tips and actionable advice to make the most out of these tools.

## Why Do Accountants and Small Business Owners Love to Hate Expense Tracking?

For accountants and small business owners, expense tracking can be a source of frustration. Here are some reasons why:

1. **Time-Consuming**: Manually entering expenses can take up valuable time that could be spent on more important tasks.
2. **Error-Prone**: It's easy to make mistakes when manually recording expenses, leading to inaccurate financial data.
3. **Data Management**: Keeping track of receipts and invoices can be overwhelming, especially for businesses with multiple employees or locations.
4. **Reporting**: Generating accurate reports from manual records can be a labor-intensive process.

## 1. Expensify

Expensify is one of the most popular expense tracking tools on the market. It offers a range of features that make it ideal for accountants and small business owners:

- **Automatic Receipt Recognition**: Expensify uses optical character recognition (OCR) to automatically capture receipt details, saving you time.
- **Multi-Currency Support**: Ideal for businesses with international operations or clients from different countries.
- **Customizable Reports**: Generate detailed reports on expenses by category, employee, and date range.
- **Integration**: Seamless integration with accounting software like QuickBooks, Xero, and Intuit QuickBooks Online.

### Practical Tips:
- **Set Up Categories**: Create categories for common expenses to streamline the process.
- **Automate Submissions**: Use the mobile app to automatically submit receipts and invoices directly from your phone or camera.
- **Regular Review**: Set up a routine to review and approve expenses regularly to keep everything organized.

## 2. Receipt Bank

Receipt Bank is another powerful expense tracking tool that offers a user-friendly interface and advanced features:

- **Quick Entry**: Quickly enter expenses using the mobile app, even if you don't have internet access.
- **Sync Across Devices**: Access your expenses from any device with an internet connection.
- **Auto-Categorization**: Receipt Bank automatically categorizes receipts based on keywords, reducing manual input.
- **Team Management**: Great for businesses with multiple users, allowing them to manage and approve expenses collectively.

### Practical Tips:
- **Enable Auto-Categorization**: Let Receipt Bank handle the category assignment to save time.
- **Set Up Teams**: If you have a team, set up a team account to streamline expense management across different locations.
- **Regular Syncs**: Ensure regular syncs between devices to avoid any data loss.

## 3. Expensify for Business

Expensify also offers an app specifically designed for small business owners:

- **Expense Types**: Includes categories for common business expenses like travel, meals, and entertainment.
- **Reimbursement Requests**: Easily request reimbursements directly from your app.
- **Custom Fields**: Add custom fields to track additional details such as project names or client information.
- **Security Features**: Robust security features ensure that your financial data is protected.

### Practical Tips:
- **Enable Custom Categories**: Create custom categories for unique business expenses.
- **Use Reimbursement Requests**: Streamline the reimbursement process by using the built-in feature.
- **Regular Audits**: Conduct regular audits to ensure compliance and accuracy in expense tracking.

Expensify also offers a version of its app specifically designed for nonprofit organizations:

- **Nonprofit Reporting**: Generate reports that comply with IRS guidelines for nonprofits.
- **Charitable Contributions Tracking**: Easily track charitable contributions and receipts.

### Practical Tips:
- **Enable Nonprofit Features**: Take advantage of features designed specifically for nonprofit organizations.

## 5. Zoho Expense

Zoho Expense is a comprehensive expense tracking tool that offers advanced features for accountants and small business owners:

- **Custom Fields**: Add custom fields to track additional details such as project names or client information.
- **Approval Workflow**: Set up an approval workflow to ensure expenses are reviewed before reimbursement.
